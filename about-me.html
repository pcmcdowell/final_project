About Me!!!

Hello. My name is Patricia and I am twenty-three years old.

I was born and raised in San Francisco on Merced Ave near Forest Hill Muni station and the very popular West Portal.



School Years

I went to St. Brendan on Ulloa St. for grammar school.

For high school I went to Washington High, and then I transferred to Terra Nova High in Pacifica when I moved to Daly City.

Now, I currently attend Skyline College, as well as Hackbright Academy.


My Favorites

My favorite color is purple and my favorite place is the beach. I love the sound of the waves crashing onto the beach.

My favorite holidays are Christmas, because it really brings my family together like no other holiday, and Halloween, because who doesn't like an excuse to get free candy?.

My favorite thing to do is either curling up on the couch with a good book and some hot chocolate in the winter, or watching a Disney movie.

My favorite weeks of the year are Shark Week, because sharks are beautiful, misunderstood creatures, and Fleet Week. The Blue Angels are amazing!

My favorite sport is soccer, because I've been playing soccer on and off since I was in Kindergarten, and my favorite team is FC Barcelona. Lionel Messi is my favorite player on this team.

I also really like the SF Giants, and occasionally I like to watch the SF 49ers.
